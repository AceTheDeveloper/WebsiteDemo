We are located here!
Leonides S. Virata Memorial School (LSVMS) is a private Catholic educational institution located in Rio Tuba, Barangay Rio Tuba, Municipality of Bataraza, Palawan, Philippines. Situated at F.P. Mamaclay Avenue, RTN Townsite, the school serves the dependents of employees from Rio Tuba Nickel Mining Corporation (RTNMC), Coral Bay Nickel Corporation (CBNC), and RTN Foundation, Inc., as well as students from neighboring communities.
